About the Author

Henry M’ule Musenge holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Zambia, as well as a Master’s and PhD from the University of Strathclyde in the UK. He has worked in various senior positions and is the author of Changing Shadows, which has been used as an English set text in Zambian schools.

His other novels include Flames of Gondola and Distorted Legacy. His fourth manuscript, Tossing the Coin, is currently under submission.
